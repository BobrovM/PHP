The supporting documents to be provided are as below.

Pre-requisites:
-A travel document, issued less than 10 years ago,
containing at least two blank pages, with a period of
validity at least 3 months longer than the date on which
you intend to leave the Schengen Area or, in the case of a
long stay, at least three months longer than the expiry date
of the visa requested. Be sure to transmit (scan) ALL PAGES
of your travel document containing visas, entry and exit stamps
or any other inscription.
-2 ID photograph.

Civil status:
-Proof of family relationship (civil registry certificates).
